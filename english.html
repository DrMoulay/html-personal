About Me

I am from Niamey, Niger, and I have always seized opportunities with determination and perseverance. I am currently pursuing my undergraduate degree in Aerospace Engineering. I have a great passion for innovation and aim to utilize my skills and experiences to make meaningful contributions to the aviation industry.

I am proficient in mechanical design, unmanned aerial vehicles, software, and web development. I have expertise and experience in design, modeling, and simulation software such as SolidWorks and Matlab. Additionally, I develop web projects using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Regarding languages, I am at the C1 level in French, Turkish, and Zarma, while I have a B2 level in English, Arabic, and Hausa. I aim to create innovative projects in the aviation and technology sectors to make a difference in the industry.

Portfolio

Tuğrul UAV

Tuğrul UAV is an autonomous unmanned aerial vehicle developed for target detection, tracking, payload transportation, and delivery missions. With the MH-115 wing profile, optimized aerodynamic structure, powerful motor system, and integrated electronic hardware, it can carry a useful load equal to 100% of its empty weight and offers high maneuverability. Thanks to mechanical design, structural and aerodynamic analyses, and software integration, it ensures reliable and stable flight performance. In the future, it is planned to enhance its capabilities with advanced sensor integration and AI-supported systems.
