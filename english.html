About Me

I am from Niamey, Niger, and I have always seized opportunities with determination and perseverance. Since middle school, I have earned prestigious scholarships; first, I was accepted into one of the top schools in my country, and then I continued my education in Turkey, developing myself in a rich international environment.

I am currently pursuing my undergraduate degree in Aerospace Engineering. I have a great passion for innovation and aim to utilize my skills and experiences to make meaningful contributions to the aviation industry.

I am proficient in mechanical design, unmanned aerial vehicles, software, and web development. I have expertise and experience in design, modeling, and simulation software such as SolidWorks and Matlab. Additionally, I develop web projects using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I hold certifications in Hang Glider piloting (35 flight hours) and RC aircraft construction and piloting (20 flight hours).

Regarding languages, I am at the C1 level in French, Turkish, and Zarma, while I have a B2 level in English, Arabic, and Hausa. I aim to create innovative projects in the aviation and technology sectors to make a difference in the industry.

Portfolio

Tuğrul UAV

Tuğrul UAV is an autonomous unmanned aerial vehicle developed for target detection, tracking, payload transportation, and delivery missions. With the MH-115 wing profile, optimized aerodynamic structure, powerful motor system, and integrated electronic hardware, it can carry a useful load equal to 100% of its empty weight and offers high maneuverability. Thanks to mechanical design, structural and aerodynamic analyses, and software integration, it ensures reliable and stable flight performance. In the future, it is planned to enhance its capabilities with advanced sensor integration and AI-supported systems.

Harakoy UAV

Harakoy UAV is a hybrid vehicle capable of operating both in the air and underwater. Despite being a fixed-wing UAV, it features a specially designed foldable wing system that enhances maneuverability underwater. This feature makes it ideal for underwater exploration, surface observation, and search and rescue operations in maritime environments. Its ability to operate in both air and water makes it a versatile platform adaptable to various mission scenarios.

Free Fall

Free Fall is a fixed-wing UAV with a modern conventional design. It offers effective thrust with its rear-mounted engine. Its aerodynamic structure, simple design, and high payload capacity make it suitable for observation, reconnaissance, and various tasks efficiently.

Captain Drone

Captain Drone is a mini drone designed for video and photo shooting in daily life. Unlike traditional drone designs, it stands out with its two-motor configuration. Its compact design and lightweight nature make it user-friendly and portable.

Stability Project

This system is developed to ensure mechanical stability around the roll axis of aircraft. While the tail provides pitch (nosedown) stability, the ailerons are designed to support stability on the roll axis. This design aims to improve flight stability, ensuring a safer and more efficient flying experience.
